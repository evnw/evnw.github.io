Naturalistic Driving App

Our team for Professional Computing (CITS3200) in 2022 was assigned to create a driving application that could be used in naturalistic driving studies by the Western Australia Centre for Road Safety Research (WACRSR). The app was designed to collect GPS and accelerometer data from the user's phone while traveling and transmit all recorded data to a Cloud Firestore database with the user's consent. In addition to the primary requirements, we incorporated some additional features, such as gathering user information, a statistics page, and a tutorial page.
