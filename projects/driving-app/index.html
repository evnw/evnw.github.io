Naturalistic Driving App

For professional computing (CITS3200) 2022, our team was tasked with developing a driving app that could be used in naturalistic driving studies by the Western Australia Centre for Road Safety Research (WACRSR). It was required to record GPS and accelerometer data from the user's phone whilst travelling. The app sends all recorded data to a Cloud Firestore database at the consent of the user. Throughout the project, some extra features were added including collection of user info, a statistics page, and a tutorial page.
