GPS-Driven Car

The digital embedded systems (ENSC3020) project for 2022 was to use a microcontroller (Arduino Nano or similar) and interface to a model car, actuating the steering and drive systems. A GPS sensor was required to be connected and a program to be implemented to drive the car to a given location and return.
